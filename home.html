Welcome

Anema Mart is a foremost trading and agriculture company in Nigeria. We currently operate our businesses and outlets in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, where we have established a foothold in the regional market, and are esteemed by our customers as the leading and trusted brand in retail.

Creating Value

Beyond achieving our business needs as a consumer goods trader, we seek to better lives by adding value to society through rural development and responsible sourcing. Through various sustainable programs, we support small-scale farmers in increasing capacity and efficiency.

Who We Are

Anema Mart is a renowned brand with interests in Retail, Mechanized Farming and Food Processing. Established in 2012, it has developed into a trusted name for its products and services. The driving thrust of our business is to find newer and better ways to serve our customers, all the time.

Online Store

Take advantage of our online store and access all our fabulous products! Our online inventory includes our selection of refrigerated, frozen, and non-perishable groceries, health and beauty products, home and gift items. Enjoy fast and easy shopping from the comfort of your homes.
