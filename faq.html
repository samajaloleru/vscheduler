How do I get started?

Once your account has been activated by your company account administrator, you can log in to the mobile app or website with the credentials that were sent to your email.

The mobile app download failed, what could be the problem?

If you are unable to download the mobile app, ensure your device is connected to a working internet connection and try again.

How does the software work with my calendar?

The application integrates with the calendar application on your mobile device when appointments are created or rescheduled via the mobile application.

Are my appointments private?

By default, no. If you choose to make an appointment private, you can enable privacy by clicking the “Private” checkbox on the dashboard or in the mobile app when creating an appointment.

I can't find my welcome email?

If you are on unable to find your welcome email, check your spam folder and ensure you mark the email as “Not Spam”. If the email is not in your spam folder, contact your company account administrator to verify an account has been created for you with your correct email address.

Can other users view or edit my appointments?

In general no. Only company administrators, front desk officers, or other users you have granted access to can create, rescheduled or remove appointments on your behalf.

How do I cancel an already booked appointment?

To remove an appointment, click on the “Action” dropdown button next to the appointment details and select the “Remove” option. You will be prompted to confirm the removal of this appointment.

I forgot my password, what should I do?

If you have forgotten your password and are unable to log in, click on the “forgot password” link on the log in screen and provide your email address. A link will be sent to your email where you can reset your password.

How do I change my password?

Passwords can be changed in either of the following ways.

1. If you are logged in, navigate to your profile page and click on the change password button. You will be prompted to change your password..

2. If you have forgotten your password and are unable to log in, click on the “forgot password” link on the log in screen and provide your email address. A link will be sent to your email where you can reset your password.

How do I reschedule an appointment?

To reschedule an appointment, click on the “Action” dropdown button next to the appointment details and select the reschedule option, this will prompt you to enter a new date and time for the appointment. Appointments that have arrived or have been deleted cannot be rescheduled.

I cannot sign in with my password, what could be the problem?

Ensure that you have correctly typed in your log in email and password and try again. If you still cannot log in contact your company account administrator to reset your password.

How long does the setup process take?

Setup duration varies with the size of the organization (ie. the number of users and locations). However, setup duration usually varies between 24 hours and 72 hours.

How do I add new users?

Users can be added one by one or by batch upload of a “.csv” file containing the list of users and their details. The .csv file format and specification is provided in the “User” section of the administrator dashboard where a sample can be downloaded. Uploads must follow the format provided in the sample.
